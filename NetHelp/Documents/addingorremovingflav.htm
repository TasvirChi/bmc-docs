Adding or Removing Flavors

  To add flavors

1.   Click + Add/-Remove flavors.
The list of available flavors is displayed,

2.   Use the arrows to move the relevant flavors to the Allowed Flavors section.

3.   Click Save.

  To remove flavors

1.   Highlight the flavors you want to remove from the restricted list.

2.   Click +Remove.

3.   Click Save.
